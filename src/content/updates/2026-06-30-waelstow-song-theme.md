Wesaþ ge Hale.

**Wælstow**, a song of echoes and ruin. It will be on our upcoming album.

From Old English, *wælstow* means "slaughter-place" or "battlefield." The song opens in the ruins of such a place, where echoes of cries and broken oaths still resound : a liminal space between memory and forgetting.
