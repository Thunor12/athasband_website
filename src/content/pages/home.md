Athas is a melodic black metal project blending atmospheric intensity with Anglo-Saxon mysticism and dark fantasy.

Rooted in themes of inner conflict and philosophical descent, it explores what lies beneath will, instinct, and belonging: what remains when these begin to fracture.

Fully prepared to transition into a live lineup, Aþas is actively seeking opportunities for live performance, as well as collaborations with labels and promoters.
