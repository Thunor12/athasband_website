**Dustsceáwung** (Old English: contemplation of dust) is the proposed title for the debut Aþas album : an arc from glory to memory, from light and revelation to what remains when the fire fades.

The record contains five songs, all intended for live performance:

- **Liegetsliht** : lightning-strike revelation; oaths to distant lords
- **Wælstow** : the slaughter-place; ancestral ruin and inherited grief
- **Scinncraft** : phantom-craft; corrupt grace and golden chalice
- **Færniþ** : fiery strife; frenzy channelled into conscious power
- **Ende** : the end; steadfast stand as the weave unravels

Guitars and drums were tracked through 2025–2026, with vocals completed at D.N.I Studio. Mixing and release preparation are underway.

Featured demo excerpt on this site: **Liegetsliht**.
