Aþas

Today we trace where our name comes from — from Old English to Proto-Germanic and the languages that still carry it.

From Old English **āþas** (pl. of **āþ**), from Proto-Germanic ***aiþaz***: "oaths." Cognate with Gothic *aiþs*, German *Eid*, and modern English *oath*.
