Athas is a melodic black metal entity blending atmospheric intensity
with Anglo-Saxon mysticism and dark fantasy.

Rooted in themes of inner conflict and philosophical descent, it explores what lies beneath will, instinct, and belonging: what remains when these begin to fracture.

With guitars and drums already recorded, Athas is currently finalizing its debut material and preparing for release.

Fully prepared to transition into a live lineup, Athas is actively seeking
opportunities for live performance, as well as collaborations with labels
and promoters.
